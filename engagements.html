Engagements

How the work is structured, and what to expect.

The Model

Every engagement starts in the same place.

Before we build anything, we map what's there. An Operational Architecture Assessment gives us a clear picture of how your business actually operates — where knowledge lives, how decisions get made, and what's missing. Everything else follows from that.

01 · Entry Point

Operational Architecture Assessment

2–3 weeks

A structured review of how your business actually operates. We interview the people who hold the knowledge, map the workflows and decision logic, and identify exactly where the gaps are.

  • Documentation inventory: what exists, what doesn't
  • Knowledge gap analysis: where the real risk is
  • AI-readiness assessment: what's ready to automate and what isn't
  • Engagement roadmap: what to build next and in what order

02 · Documentation

Documentation Sprint

4–6 weeks

We document a specific domain of your business — workflows, pricing logic, decision rules, communication standards — from the ground up. The output is a structured knowledge base, not a binder on a shelf.

  • SOP library for the target domain
  • Pricing and reference data in structured format
  • Judgment rules and exception logic written down
  • Review and validation with the team

03 · Implementation

AI Implementation

Scoped per engagement

With documentation in place, we build the AI layer. This could be a document production suite, an AI Q&A system trained on your SOPs, or automated workflows that run without manual input. The scope depends on what the Assessment and Sprint uncovered.

  • Tool design based on documented knowledge
  • Build and deployment
  • Team training and handoff
  • 30-day post-launch support
