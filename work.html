Case Study

Construction OS

A residential GC in Boston needed to stop rebuilding every document from scratch. We built the operating system their business had never had.

Client

High-end residential general contractor. 15–20 active projects. 5-person PM team. $8M–$15M annual project volume.

Problem

Project knowledge scattered across people, email threads, and file systems. Every document built from scratch. New PMs needed months before they were productive. The owner was in every conversation because no one else had the context to answer client questions.

What We Built

Construction OS — an AI operating system built on 160+ SOPs and a 1,258-line pricing database from real project data. Includes a full document production suite: contracts, estimates, change orders, and client communications — all generated from structured knowledge, not blank templates.

Outcome

The team produces contract-ready documents in minutes. New PMs are productive on day one. The owner has stepped back from document production entirely.

Preliminary Estimates

½ day → 1 hour

Change Order Turnaround
